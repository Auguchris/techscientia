Welcome to Techscientia Consultancy


We are a Research & advisory firm based in Kigali, Rwanda. Our work is focused on strategic consultancies aiming at supporting Public and Private sector Development in the areas of Agriculture , Environment, Health, and Education

Founded in 2015, Techscientia ltd is a Rwandan research and advisory firm striving to build the next generation of leaders in socio-economic research

Dr.Clement Urinzwenimana

Principal of Techscientia

I am a Rwandan agriculture expert. I have over 18 years working with national extension and research systems. More than 13 years demonstrated experience in the design of agricultural policies and programs in the area of seed production and supply, poverty reduction, food and nutrition security. Demonstrated working experience in Commodity value chains, Agriculture finance and marketing systems, reducing post-harvest handling and sustainable cooling systems and Public private partnerships. Extensive experience in gender and climate smart agriculture development. Seven years of teaching experience in high learning institutions as Senior Lecturer.. ... As agriculture technical advisor of the Ministry of Finance and Economic Planning in Rwanda, I have supported the National Development Planning & Research department to develop a high quality public investment program, based on adopted long-term, medium-term and annual public investment objectives and performance indicators in the Agriculture sector with the specific key Tasks of (i) ensure all projects proposals submitted by the agriculture sector follow the public investment policies, regulations and procedures (ii) Analyse policies and strategies to ascertain bottlenecks affecting public investment in agriculture sector (iii) participate in the preparation of ToRs for pre-feasibility / feasibility studies for agriculture projects (iv) Conduct preliminary cost benefit analysis, financial and economic analysis and risk assessment of all pre-screened projects (v) Reviewing agriculture PPP projects submitted by RDB and advise the PPP Steering Committee before approval and (x) Advise the public Investment Committee (PIC) on the proposed short and medium term public investment plans in the agriculture sector..
